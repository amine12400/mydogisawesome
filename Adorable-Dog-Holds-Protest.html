Adorable Dog Holds Protest Signs That Say What Dogs Are Really Thinking

If you spend a lot of time on the internet, you’ve probably already seen an adorable black and tan pup who is trending for their adorable way of shedding some light on what dogs are really thinking.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Baboy (@dogwithsign)

The little dog has an Instagram titled, “dogwithsign” and has the very appropriate tagline, “If you don’t sit for something, you’ll never get the treat.”

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Baboy (@dogwithsign)

And there are already more than 10,000 people following the little dog – who so far has shared thoughts about mailmen, people who cut up dog treats, and much more. These hilarious protests are put onto cardboard signs that the little dog then poses with in its mouth throughout New York.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Baboy (@dogwithsign)

It all began with “Stop pretending to throw the ball,” which then was captioned as “you know who you are.” Some of the more popular protests include “Stop blaming your farts on me,” and “Stop letting humans judge dog shows.” A few of the signs have even been bits of advice to other dogs like, “Don’t trust the mailman,” or “Toilets are just big water bowls.”

These signs are so hilarious, they are guaranteed to have dog owners everywhere laughing because of their authentic ring.
