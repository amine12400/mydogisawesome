The Golden Retriever Dances To Celebrate The Arrival Of His Dinner

Baker Barnes is a golden retriever who likes carrots, swimming and lakes. But he also likes dinner, every night he dances to celebrate the arrival of his meal.

His owner recorded him and his video began to gain attention after it was shared on Twitter and Instagram.

“This is Baker. He dances when it’s dinner time. 14/10 top-tier tippy taps,” wrote ‘We Rate Dogs’ while sharing the clip.
